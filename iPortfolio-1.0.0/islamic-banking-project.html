‚ú® Key Features

Shariah Compliance Screening

Advanced screening engine to evaluate stocks, funds, and investments against Islamic finance principles. Real-time compliance monitoring with detailed reports on debt ratios, revenue sources, and business activities.

Portfolio Analytics

Comprehensive portfolio tracking with Shariah-compliant performance metrics, risk assessment, and diversification analysis. Includes Zakat calculations and purification requirements.

Profit-Loss Sharing Calculator

Interactive calculator for Mudarabah and Musharakah contracts. Compute profit distribution ratios, capital contributions, and returns based on Islamic partnership principles.

Sukuk Performance Tracker

Real-time tracking and analysis of Sukuk (Islamic bonds) performance. Compare yields, maturities, and risk profiles across different Sukuk structures (Ijarah, Murabaha, Wakalah).

Halal Investment Screener

Powerful screening tool to discover halal investment opportunities across global markets. Filter by industry, geography, market cap, and Shariah compliance scores.

Risk Management Dashboard

Islamic finance-aligned risk assessment tools. Evaluate Gharar (uncertainty), Maysir (gambling), and Riba (interest) exposure in investment portfolios.
